instagram

february 24, 2025

I miss Instagram spam 2018-2019 it was so much fun. Users like, Nateclips would do Instagram raids where you would send in a live, and the host would tell you what to say. For a pg-13 example, "congrats on failing a test". Then, the host ends the live. finally, everyone goes and spams the live sent in. Having to explain it makes it sound so lame now. Time really is a liar. Maybe I don't miss it, am just tired of how aware everyone is of their public image. All your pictures need to be aesthetically pleasing and matching while also, being mysterious and not trying too hard. You can see the people who put in so much effort (I'm talking to you model, DJ, clothing brand, and producer guys) but no real hate too them; everyone cares about image.
